Bio

Audioslave was an American rock supergroup formed in Glendale, California in 2001. The 4-man group consisted of members from the band Soundgarden and Rage Against the Machine.

The Members

  • Chris Cornell (Soundgarden, Singer)
  • Tom Morello (RAtM, lead guitar)
  • Tim Commerford (RAtM, Bass)
  • Brad Wilk (RAtM, Drums)
